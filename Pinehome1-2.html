Pine

We are a community of data analysts and researchers that work together to create timely and impactful analytics that keep our partners and the wider Web3 ecosystem up-to-date and informed.

What can Pine do for you

Our Services

Protocol Activity Analysis
Our community of analysts creates dashboards and tools to show the activity of a protocol in terms of users, volume and other key performance indicators.
Web3 Sector Report
Our community of analysts creates a comprehensive reports of the trends, user activity, and top players in a web3 sector or niche.
Flash Analysis
Our community of analysts creates data-driven insights with a fast turnaround about any question that can be answered using on chain data.
Data-set analysis
Our community of analysts creates data-driven insights on large data-set to discover trends and insight./div>

Each week Pine will run a joke race on the Polygon blockchain using jokedao.io this race will allow any person who has created on-chain analysis in the week of the race to submit their work. Once all the analysis is submitted for the week, the holders of Cantina-Pines NFTs and Pine Champion soulbound tokens will get to vote on the submission that they think is the best. Voting power is 1 if the voter is holding a “Cantina-Pines” NFT or soulbound “Pine champion” Token.

The highest votes submission (in case of tie, tie break is done in the discord server of only the top submissions) will receive a soulbound “Pine Champion” token and newly minted “Cantina-Pines” NFT. In addition to the NFT rewards for the winner of the race, members of the discord will be encouraged to interact with and like a social media post of the winners submission.
