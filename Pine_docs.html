Cantina-pines NFT

What can you do with a Pine?

The Cantina-pines NFT allows its holder to participate in the weekly analysts contest as a voter, and choose the topic of the contest by voting in the bribe market. Each week the Cantina-pines holder will receive a portion of the bribe if they voted in both the bribe market and the contest for the week.

Why hold a Pine?

Pine is a DAO that is looking to put great analysts, reseahers, educators, and developers in the position to elevate each other's work and create value through coordination/collaboration. Holders of Pine will be incentivised to create value and offer opportunities to each other, and Pine will continually create streamlined ways for members of the DAO to create value for each other.

How do you get a Pine?
  • Get the most votes in a weekly contest
  • Buy one on the Secondary market
  • Cantina-pines can hold a referendum to mint new Cantina-pines to further Pine’s mission

Weekly Joke Race

What is the weekly joke race?

Each week Pine will hold a joke race if there is no bribe then the topic will be open analysis, education, and research in the web3 space. If there is a bribe the briber will pick what the topic of the analysis, education, and research must be focused on. Content that is provided to the contest must be created in the week of the contest or the week prior.

What are the steps of the weekly joke race?

The first part of the contest allows any person to submit content that they have created that fits the criteria for the contest. Once the submission window ends wallets that were holding Cantina-pines NFTs may vote on what submission is the best (wallets that do not hold the Cantina-pines NFT, but hold the Pine Champion Soulbound token may also vote).

The submission that receives the most votes at the end of the voting period wins. The winner will receive a newly minted Cantina-pines NFT and a Pine Champion Soulbound token. All contests are held at jokedao.io

Soulbond Tokens

What are the Pine soulbond tokens?

Currently there are three types of Soulbond token on PINE Champion, PINE Citizen, and PINE Contender. Users that receive these tokens can not transfer them to other addresses.

How do you get Pine soulbond tokens?

The PINE Champion token is given to users when they win one of the weekly Joke races. The PINE Citizen token is given to voters when they vote for a contestant. The PINE Contender token is given to users when they submit to a weekly contest and come in the top 5.

What do the Pine soulbond tokens do?
  • PINE Champion
    • Discord channel
    • Joke race voting without Cantina-Pine NFT
  • PINE Citizen
    • If a user has 5 or more then PINE Citizen they gain 2 votes in a contest
  • PINE Contestant
    • If a user has 5 or more then PINE Challenger they are in the running to get minted a pine every 2 months.

Bribe Market

What is the Pine bribe market?

Currently the bribe market is being built. When the bribe market is live, anybody will be able to propose a topic for a future weeking contest along with a bribe. Holders of the Cantina-pines NFT will be able to vote on what bribe/topics they want. At the end of the voting period the topic with the most votes wins and the bribe is distributed between the weekly contestants, voters, and PINE.

What would an example bribe look like?

“Topic: week 7 submissions should show users and transaction activity of SPELL and MIM activity on Ethereum in 2022
Bribe Contestant: 100 MATIC 1st, 50 MATIC 2nd
Bribe Pine Voters: 200 MATIC”
