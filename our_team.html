Who we are?

Pine Analytics was founded by three data analysts from the greater Boston area who became fascinated with the possibilities of smart contracts and blockchain technology in 2020. They combined their expertise in data analysis with their interest in the space and joined forces with Flipside Crypto to create insightful analysis and reports on blockchain activity.

After nearly two years of delivering meaningful and impactful dashboards and reports using blockchain data, the team decided to create Pine Analytics, a consulting firm specializing in providing valuable analytics solutions for the Web3 community. With a dedication to delivering impactful insights in a timely manner, Pine Analytics helps its partners make informed decisions backed by data, enabling success and growth in the dynamic and constantly evolving Web3 ecosystem.

Our analytics comunity

At Pine Analytics, we foster a vibrant community of skilled data analysts who are actively engaged in the Web3 space. Every week, we host a public contest to recognize the best data analysis in the crypto/Web3 space. The winners receive a coveted NFT and are added to our growing community of talented analysts. With this community, we are able to continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in data analysis and bring fresh perspectives to our client engagements. Whether it's through brainstorms or contests, our community is dedicated to exploring innovative ways to look at data and drive meaningful insights for the Web3 ecosystem.
